# Hospital Operations & Cost Efficiency Analysis (Excel Dashboard)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes hospital data to uncover insights into **revenue generation, patient trends, and treatment efficiency**.
Using Excel Pivot Tables and an interactive dashboard, the analysis highlights key operational and financial patterns across hospitals and medical conditions.

---

## Objectives

* Identify top revenue-generating hospitals
* Analyze patient distribution across admission types
* Evaluate **average length of stay (ALOS)** across diseases
* Measure **cost efficiency (Cost per Day)**
* Understand the impact of **BMI on hospitalization duration**

---

## Tools & Techniques

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Slicers (Interactive Filters)
* Calculated Metrics:

  * Cost per Day = Billing Amount / Days Hospitalized
  * BMI Categories
* Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Summary:

  * Total Revenue
  * Total Patients
  * Average Length of Stay
* Revenue by Hospital (Sorted for performance comparison)
* Average Stay by Medical Condition
* Cost Efficiency across diseases
* Admission Type distribution
* BMI Impact Analysis
* Interactive Filters (Hospital, Gender, BMI Category)

---

## Key Insights

* Revenue contribution varies significantly across hospitals, indicating uneven patient distribution
* Average hospital stay remains relatively stable (~15–17 days), suggesting standardized treatment timelines
* Cost per day varies across medical conditions, reflecting differences in treatment complexity
* Underweight patients show a higher average hospital stay, indicating potential health vulnerability
* Cancer treatments show relatively higher cost per day, suggesting intensive care requirements

---

## Business Impact

* Helps hospitals identify **high-revenue centers**
* Supports **resource planning** using patient trends
* Enables **cost optimization** by analyzing treatment efficiency
* Provides insights for **patient care strategy improvement**
